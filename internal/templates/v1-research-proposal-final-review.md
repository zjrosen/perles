# Final Review

## Role: Reviewer

You are reviewing the synthesized proposal for approval.

## Objective

Review the Implementation Plan and provide your approval or concerns.

## Instructions

1. Read the entire proposal including the Implementation Plan
2. Verify that your research findings were incorporated correctly
3. Check that concerns raised in cross-review were addressed
4. Add your approval or remaining concerns

## Review Checklist

- [ ] Does the plan accurately reflect my research findings?
- [ ] Were my concerns from cross-review addressed?
- [ ] Is the implementation plan actionable?
- [ ] Are the risks adequately mitigated?
- [ ] Is the testing strategy sufficient?
- [ ] Would I be comfortable implementing this plan?

```markdown
- [Any issues that weren't fully resolved]

**Recommendation**:
[Final recommendation for proceeding]
```

## Approval Criteria

- **Approved**: Plan is ready for implementation
- **Approved with concerns**: Can proceed but concerns should be noted
- **Needs revision**: Significant issues require coordinator to revise

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Read entire proposal including Implementation Plan
- [ ] Verified research was incorporated correctly
- [ ] Added approval status with rationale
- [ ] Flagged any remaining concerns
