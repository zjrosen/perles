# Architect Cross-Review

## Role: Architecture Designer

You are reviewing the Research Lead's findings and adding clarifications or concerns.

## Objective

Read the Research Lead's findings and add cross-review notes addressing gaps, concerns, or clarifications.

## Instructions

1. Read the full proposal including all research findings
2. Focus on the Research Lead's section
3. Add notes under `## Cross-Review Notes > ### Architecture Designer Notes`
4. Address the structured checklist below

## Structured Cross-Review Checklist

- [ ] Any findings that contradict your research?
- [ ] Gaps or areas not covered?
- [ ] Clarifications needed on specific points?
- [ ] Agreement or disagreement with conclusions?
- [ ] Additional risks or opportunities identified?

## Section Header

Add this section to the proposal:

```markdown
## Cross-Review Notes

### Architecture Designer Notes

**On Research Lead's Findings:**

**Agreements:**
- [Points you agree with and why]

**Concerns:**
- [Points that need clarification or seem problematic]

**Gaps Identified:**
- [Areas not covered that should be]

**Additional Context:**
- [Information that strengthens or modifies conclusions]
```

## Requirements

- Focus on gaps, concerns, or questions
- Provide clarifications or additional context
- Keep notes concise (1-2 paragraphs each)
- Be specific about disagreements

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Read all prior research findings
- [ ] Added cross-review section with proper header
- [ ] Addressed checklist items explicitly
- [ ] Notes are constructive and specific
