# Risk Analyst Cross-Review

## Role: Risk/Trade-off Analyst

You are reviewing all prior findings and adding cross-review notes.

## Objective

Read the Research Lead's and Architecture Designer's findings and add cross-review notes addressing gaps, concerns, or clarifications.

## Instructions

1. Read the full proposal including all research findings and Architect's cross-review
2. Focus on both Research Lead's and Architecture Designer's sections
3. Add notes under `## Cross-Review Notes > ### Risk Analyst Notes`
4. Address the structured checklist below

## Structured Cross-Review Checklist

- [ ] Any findings that contradict your research?
- [ ] Gaps or areas not covered?
- [ ] Clarifications needed on specific points?
- [ ] Agreement or disagreement with conclusions?
- [ ] Additional risks or opportunities identified?

## Section Header

Add to the Cross-Review Notes section:

```markdown
### Risk Analyst Notes

**On Research Lead's Findings:**
- [Comments, concerns, agreements]

**On Architecture Designer's Findings:**
- [Comments, concerns, agreements]

**Risks from Proposed Approach:**
- [New risks identified from the design]

**Gaps Identified:**
- [Areas needing more attention]
```

## Requirements

- Review both prior research sections
- Identify any risks from the proposed architecture
- Keep notes concise and focused
- Be specific about disagreements

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Read all prior research and cross-review notes
- [ ] Added Risk Analyst Notes section
- [ ] Identified any new risks from proposed design
- [ ] Notes are constructive and specific
